## What Changes

**Resources page filter row:** Replace the five pillar pills with: All, Jobs, Housing, Childcare, Transportation, Health, Money, Legal, Education. Horizontally scrollable if they don't all fit on screen.

**Resource cards:** The pillar label on each card (currently showing "WELLNESS", "RESILIENCE", etc.) now shows the new category name in the category's color.

**Database:** Update the pillar enum (or whatever field stores the category) on the resources table to use the eight new values: jobs, housing, childcare, transportation, health, money, legal, education. Remove the old five values.

**Admin forms:** The "Pillar" dropdown on the resource creation/edit form becomes "Category" with the eight new options.
